Privacy Policy

Crown Climb

Last updated: February 17, 2026

Overview

Crown Climb is a single-player offline card game. Your privacy is important to us, and this policy explains how the app handles your data.

Data Collection

Crown Climb does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The app:

  • Does not require an account or login
  • Does not use analytics or tracking services
  • Does not contain advertising
  • Does not access your contacts, location, camera, microphone, or any other device sensors
  • Does not use cookies or similar technologies

Local Storage

The app stores your game settings (such as bot turn speed) locally on your device using UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device and is automatically removed when you delete the app.

Apple Game Center

Crown Climb integrates with Apple Game Center for leaderboards and achievements. When you are signed in to Game Center on your device, the app may send your scores and achievement progress to Apple. This data is handled by Apple in accordance with Apple's Privacy Policy. Crown Climb does not collect or store your Game Center data independently.

Third-Party Services

Other than Apple Game Center, Crown Climb does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs.

Children's Privacy

Crown Climb does not collect any data from any user, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the revised version will be posted here with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the GitHub repository.
